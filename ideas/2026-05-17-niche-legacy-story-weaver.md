# Niche Legacy Story Weaver

## Concept
A service where users upload family photos, voice notes, documents, anecdotes. AI agents turn them into beautiful legacy books, videos, podcasts, or interactive memory vaults.

**Target:** Families, communities, gifts.

**Monetization:** $49-299 per project, subscriptions, white-label.

## Execution
- Agents: Intake, Research/Story, Creative (text/image/video), Assembly, QA, Delivery.
- Tech: CrewAI/LangGraph + Next.js frontend, Supabase, generation APIs.
- MVP: Photo-to-PDF pipeline.

Human role: Oversight, marketing, sales.

Pushed on 2026-05-17.
